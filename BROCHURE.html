The Patient Journey

From walk-in to follow-up — fully tracked

The system follows every patient through their entire experience at your hospital. Nothing falls through the cracks.

1

Patient Arrives — Token Issued in Seconds

The receptionist enters the patient's phone number. If they've visited before, their name and record auto-fill instantly. A token is issued, a slip is printed, and the patient joins the digital queue — all in under 15 seconds.

Token Queue System
2

Token Called — Patient Gets WhatsApp Notification

When the patient's turn arrives, "Call Next" is clicked. Their token number and name appear on the full-screen TV display in the waiting room, a voice announcement plays, and a WhatsApp message is sent to their phone — automatically.

TV Display + WhatsApp
3

Admitted? Card Issued and Sent to Their Phone

If the patient is admitted, an IPD Room Card (IPD-XXXX) is created with their ward, room, bed, doctor, and diagnosis. If it's an OPD visit, an OPD Visit Card (OPD-XXXX) is issued with a 14-day validity. Both are printable in credit-card format and shareable via WhatsApp in one click.

In-Patient Card System
4

Treatment Done — Invoice Generated and Shared

At billing, the receptionist selects pre-configured service items (consultation, lab, surgery, room charges) and adds any custom items. An auto-numbered invoice (INV-XXXX) is generated, printed on A4, and can be shared via WhatsApp to the patient's phone immediately.

Hospital Billing
5

Medicines Dispensed — Stock Auto-Updated

The pharmacist searches for medicines by name, adds them to a pharmacy bill (PHRM-XXXX), and saves. Stock is automatically deducted — no manual stock update needed. If any medicine is running low, the dashboard shows an alert before it becomes a problem.

Pharmacy ERP
6

Review Requested — Reputation Built Automatically

After billing or discharge, the receptionist sends a review request via WhatsApp. The patient submits their rating and feedback. The admin approves it, and it appears on your public website — building your hospital's reputation with zero extra effort.

Patient Reviews + Website
