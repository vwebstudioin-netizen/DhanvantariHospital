Digital System Features

Everything the hospital needs to manage patients digitally

🏠

Hospital Website

Professional website with all 8 departments, 15 services, doctors, appointments, patient portal, blog, gallery, and reviews.

🎫

Patient Token Queue

Token system for walk-in patients. Reception issues tokens. Doctor calls next. Patients track position from phone.

📺

Waiting Room TV Display

Full-screen display showing current serving token with audio chime. Real-time updates — plug into any TV or monitor.

📄

In-Patient Cards (14-Day)

Issue patient admission cards with ward, room, doctor, diagnosis. Auto-expires in 14 days. Print 85×54mm card format.

📋

Invoice & Billing

Create itemised hospital invoices — consultation, surgery, medicines, room charges, lab tests. Print or WhatsApp share.

📱

WhatsApp Notifications

Token-called alerts, admission card details, appointment reminders — all sent automatically via Twilio WhatsApp.

🤖

WhatsApp Bot

Patients message the hospital number. Bot auto-replies with appointment info, card details, queue status. Keywords: APPOINTMENT, CARD, STATUS, HELP.

🎁

Festive Wishes

Send personalised WhatsApp greetings to all patients in one click — Diwali, Eid, New Year, Pongal, and more.

💊

Pharmacy Inventory

Medicine catalog, stock management, dispense tracking, supplier management, low stock alerts, and expiry reports.

3-Role Staff Access System

Single login page — each staff member sees only what they need

Admin

Hospital Administrator

Full access — website content, all patients, queue, pharmacy, billing, staff roles, analytics, settings.

Pharmacist

Pharmacist

Pharmacy-only panel — medicines, stock in/out, dispensing, suppliers, expiry reports. No other access.

Receptionist

Receptionist

Desk panel — issue tokens, in-patient cards, invoices, festive wishes. Token queue included.
